ML-SPL Query Templates

fit command

Classifiers

- DecisionTreeClassifier, LogisticRegression, RandomForestClassifier, SVM, BernoulliNB, GaussianNB
| inputlookup {input_file}
| head {num_inputs}
| fit {algo} {target} from {fields} into model_{algo}_t{num_inputs}_f{num_features}

Regressors

- DecisionTreeRegressor, KernelRidge, LinearRegression, RandomForestRegressor, Lasso, ElasticNet, Ridge
| inputlookup {input_file}
| head {num_inputs}
| fit {algo} {target} from {fields} into model_{algo}_t{num_inputs}_f{num_features}

- FieldSelector
| inputlookup {input_file}

| fit {algo} {target} from {fields} into model_{algo}_t{num_inputs}_f{num_features}

| inputlookup {input_file}

- TFIDF
| inputlookup {input_file}
| head {num_inputs}
| fit {algo} {fields} max_features={num_features} into model_{algo}_t{num_inputs}_f{num_features}
